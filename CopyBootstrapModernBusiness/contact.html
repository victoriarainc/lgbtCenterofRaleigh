Parking


Street Parking

Street Parking is available and ranges from 30 minute spaces to 2 hour spaces.

Street Parking time limits ARE STRICTLY ENFORCED

Dedicated Parking

There is dedicated parking behind the center. A parking permit is required to park here and you may obtain a pass from the front desk at the Center

Volunteers

While staffing the Center, volunteers will have access to dedicated parking behind the Center. These spaces require a parking permit that is available at the front desk and must be returned before your shift is complete

Special Events

Special Events that occur during the week after 6pm (Monday-Thursday) and most of the time on weekends, you may park in the back gravel lot behind the Center. You will not be towed during these times. Please be sure to tell the attendant that you are "Going to an event at the LGBT Center."

Important

Unless otherwise advised by James or Kelly, no one is to park in the back lot without a parking pass or outside of the designated times above. The Center is not responsible for your vehicle or fines/impoundment fees that result in deviation from these guidelines.
