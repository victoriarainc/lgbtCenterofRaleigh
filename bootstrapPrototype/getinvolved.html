Volunteer Opportunities - General

The LGBT Center of Raleigh offers many opportunities for involvement. Volunteers undertake a variety of tasks and have a key role in staffing the Center. Typical responsibilities include welcoming visitors, answering phone calls, responding to inquiries about programs or the Center, assisting with our library, and participating in projects. Special events often need the support from volunteers to set up, run and breakdown.

If interested, please fill out our volunteer profile to attend an orientation and learn more about your role at the Center. During the session, you will learn more about our programs, meet staff members and key volunteers. Volunteer orientation is typically held on the first Saturday of each month at 11:00 AM.

Volunteer Opportunities - The Library

The Library is also currently looking for help with the special projects listed below; please contact library@lgbtcenterofraleigh.com to learn more.

  • MLS candidates at UNC and NCCU seeking a library setting for internships/practicums
  • Local librarians and book lovers to participate on the Library Steering Committee
  • Community members to consult on selection of Spanish-language LGBTQ materials
  • Local writers to participate in “Meet the Author” events throughout the year
  • Catalogers to process new donations of books and DVDs
  • Volunteers to set-up and breakdown our annual Out! Raleigh Book Sale
  • Book club leaders to foster discussion on a variety of topics, such as banned books and award-winning LGBTQ literature

ReachOUT NC




ReachOUT NC aims to band together and mobilize the LGBT community and its allies by providing volunteer opportunities for its members to improve the lives in our community by promoting goodwill.

“There is nothing to do here besides going to the bars and clubs.” How many times have you heard that? ReachOUT is a brand new organization for the LGBT community and its allies. We are a group that comes together on a regular basis to do good things for the areas in which we live. You may find us preparing meals for the families of children being treated in a hospital, building a house for a needy family, or beautifying a park. While the projects may vary from month to month, they will always be focused on making a positive difference in the world and having a great time doing it.

We welcome ALL volunteers regardless of sexual orientation, gender identity, race, color, religion, age, and ability.
