LGBT Library

  1. Since the Library opened in October 2011, the collection has approximately 3,000 volumes focused on and celebrating LGBT life. The Library’s collection development priorities are aimed at supporting the resource and information needs of the Center’s vital outreach programs such as SAGE Raleigh, Youth Coffeehouse, Affirming Faith Forum, and Transgender Initiative. The Library is also a welcoming place for family members and allies of LGBT people. The Library is the Winner of the American Library Association 2014 “Light the Way Grant.”

Become a Member

Register for a library card by filling out this form. Once completed, please drop it off at our location during regular business hours for processing. Proof of current residence in the NC Triangle area and an active email address or phone number are required. To borrow books, a one-time membership fee of $5 per individual is required. Youth under the age of 18 can check out books for free.

Disc Club

Disc Club membership is open to Wake, Durham, and Orange county residents age 18 or older currently registered with a valid LGBT Center of Raleigh Library card. The one-time $5 Disc Club membership fee is non-refundable. Disc Club membership forms are available at the Center for processing by a Library volunteer during regular business hours.
